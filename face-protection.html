We stock a variety of face protection items with plenty of options such as face masks, face shields and safety glasses and goggles. You can buy promotional disposable face masks from us in varying degrees of quality and certification. We have both TGA and FDA certified face masks, with all of our TGA ones being medical grade. We are a direct importer so we control the quality from original purchase right through to final sale, guaranteeing you the quality you would expect.

We sell bulk face masks in a 3 ply construction. They are our cheaper option, but still, give a 90% level of protection to airborne pathogens. If you are looking for higher quality face masks, we also offer KN95 grade in two certifications - FDA or medical grade TGA approved. We have the SDS sheets available if needed, and we stock locally for fast delivery to anywhere in Australia.

Our disposable masks are intended for personal use and should be disposed of once used. If you are in an office environment, you can offer them to staff to help them feel safer, or if you are in retail, again it is an excellent option to give these to your team as they deal with client’s throughout the day. Whatever your need, if you don’t see what you want in our offerings, please don’t hesitate to contact us as we have huge resources for sourcing almost anything that is required.
